Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time, known for his exceptional consistency, record-breaking performances, and aggressive leadership style. Kohli made his debut in 2008 and has since become a dominant force in all three formats of the game, holding numerous records including the most centuries in ODI cricket. Early Career and Rise to Prominence: Kohli made his ODI debut in 2008 and quickly established himself as a key player for India. He led India to victory in the 2008 Under-19 Cricket World Cup and was part of the 2011 World Cup winning team. Dominating the Record Books: Kohli holds the record for the most ODI centuries and is second in the list of most international centuries scored. He is also the highest run-scorer in the Indian Premier League (IPL).
